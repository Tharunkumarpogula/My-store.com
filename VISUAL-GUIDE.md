##🎨 Recommendations

### **Product Images:**
- **Size:** 300x300 pixels (square)
- **Format:** JPG/PNG
- **Quality:** High resolution, well-lit
- **Background:** White or transparent preferred

### **Category Images:**
- **Size:** 300x200 pixels (landscape)
- **Content:** Representative of category
- **Style:** Clean, professional

### **Hero Banner:**
- **Size:** 1200x400 pixels (wide)
- **Content:** Lifestyle or product showcase
- **Text:** None (text is overlaid)

### **Team Photos:**
- **Size:** 200x200 pixels (square)
- **Format:** JPG
- **Style:** Professional headshots

##🛠️ How to Replace Images

1. **Prepare your images** in the recommended sizes
2. **Replace the existing files** in the images folder
3. **Use descriptive filenames** (e.g., "wireless-headphones.jpg")
4. **Test on different devices** to ensure quality

##📱 Mobile View Considerations

All images are automatically responsive, but for best mobile experience:
- Keep file sizes under 200KB each
- Use compressed JPG format
- Maintain aspect ratios for proper display
